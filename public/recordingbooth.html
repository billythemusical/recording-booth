You may want to consider the following questions or just tell your story:
1. When were you or your family member at the NYS Training School?
2. What was happening in your life and in your family before you were sent away?
3. What was your first day there like?
4. Best memory? Worst memory?
5. Is there a story of one person who has deeply affected you and your life?
6. Voice - how did you find your voice and/or start to use it?
7. What word/s has someone used to define you? And what word/s would you use to define yourself?
8. What does ‘incorrigible’ mean to you?
9. What did the exhibition bring up for you?
